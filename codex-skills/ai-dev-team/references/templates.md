# Handoff Templates

Every artifact must include:

- `objective`
- `inputs`
- `constraints`
- `artifacts_out`
- `done_criteria`
- `open_questions`
- `escalation_trigger`
- `loop_objective`
- `iteration_count`
- `progress_evidence`
- `stop_condition`
- `human_checkpoint`
 
## TaskBrief

Add:

- `priority`
- `requested_deadline`
- `workflow_path`
- `current_owner`
- `next_owner`
- `risk_level`

## SpecDoc

Add:

- `target_user`
- `user_scenarios`
- `acceptance_criteria`
- `non_goals`
- `edge_conditions`
- `dependencies`

## TechPlan

Add:

- `architecture_changes`
- `interfaces_impacted`
- `data_model_changes`
- `implementation_steps`
- `risk_analysis`
- `test_strategy`
- `rollout_notes`

## ImplementationPacket

Add:

- `code_changes`
- `tests_added_or_updated`
- `manual_verification_steps`
- `known_risks`
- `deviations_from_plan`
- `artifacts_to_review`

## QAVerdict

Add:

- `status`
- `test_matrix`
- `evidence`
- `regressions_found`
- `residual_risks`
- `recommended_next_step`

## ReleaseChecklist

Add:

- `qa_status`
- `config_changes`
- `migration_plan`
- `rollback_plan`
- `monitoring_watch_items`
- `release_notes`
- `release_recommendation`
